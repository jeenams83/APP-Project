For Parents · Why This Matters

Why a 4-Year Plan Matters

The reasons a consistent, grade-by-grade approach makes all the difference.

1

It's a Holistic Evaluation, Not a Score Test

GPA, activities, leadership, essays, and recommendations are all weighed together. High scores alone don't get in — students with a distinct identity do. We design every element, not just scores.

2

US Admissions Starts in Grade 9

Colleges see all four years of transcripts. Even 9th-grade grades stay on the record, and only activities built over 3–4 years carry weight. We set a 4-year roadmap from Grade 9.

3

Each Grade's Tasks Are Different

Grade 9: foundation · 10: building · 11: the critical year · 12: apply, visa, departure. Miss a grade's window and it can't be undone.

4

Hard to Do Alone from Korea

Common App, I-20, SEVIS, F-1 visa, and school-specific deadlines and documents all differ. Scholarship strategy for international students is entirely different. One missed detail can cost an admitted student their place.

5

Time · Strategy · Objectivity

Time: we handle the complex admin and deadlines. Strategy: we find where your child shines brightest. Objectivity: clear analysis balanced with warm encouragement — with transparent reports every grade.

6

The GW Consulting Promise

We don't guarantee admission. We promise to draw out your child's full potential and walk beside them for four years, so they can aim for the best university they can reach.
